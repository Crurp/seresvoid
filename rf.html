RF and Digital Signal Processing

In adaptive signal processing, the goal is to adapt a filter's parameters to optimize the performance in dynamic environments, such as noisy signals.

Adaptive Filter Design

Adaptive filters, such as the Least Mean Squares (LMS) and Recursive Least Squares (RLS) algorithms, are crucial in scenarios like noise cancellation, echo suppression, and channel equalization. These filters can adjust their coefficients based on the input signal, making them suitable for real-time applications like wireless communication systems and radar signal processing.
