Quantum Information Science

Quantum Machine Learning (QML) aims to combine quantum computing and machine learning to accelerate training and improve accuracy.

Applications

Quantum neural networks (QNNs) are an exciting area where quantum circuits are used to improve the learning capability of neural networks. Unlike classical models, QNNs utilize quantum superposition and entanglement to process information more efficiently, making them a powerful tool for complex pattern recognition tasks, such as image and speech processing, in quantum computing environments.
